Our Programmes

Feeding Schemes

We operate community kitchens and distribute grocery hampers to families in need. Our feeding programmes reach Phoenix, KwaMashu, Tongaat, and Fraser.

How it works: Volunteers pack hampers and cook meals weekly. We also provide secure storage for essential goods.

Skills Training

We offer free, accredited courses in:

  • CV Writing
  • English Literacy
  • Computer Literacy

These skills empower individuals to find employment and become self‑sufficient.

Trauma Sanctuary Housing

Our safe house provides temporary accommodation for survivors of abuse and violence. Residents receive counselling, legal support, and assistance reintegrating into the community.

Future Plans: Cape Town Centre

We are excited to expand our model to Manenberg, Mitchells Plain, and Lavender Hill in Cape Town. Stay tuned for updates.
