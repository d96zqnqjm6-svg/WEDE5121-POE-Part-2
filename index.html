Welcome to Aurora Hands of Hope

Aurora Hands of Hope is a registered non‑profit organisation based in Durban, South Africa. We provide essential feeding schemes, trauma sanctuary housing, and accredited skills training to uplift the most vulnerable in our communities.

Our Programmes

  • Feeding Schemes: Weekly grocery hampers and community kitchens.
  • Skills Training: CV writing, English literacy, and computer literacy.
  • Trauma Support: Safe shelter for survivors of abuse and violence.
