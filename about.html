About Aurora Hands of Hope

Our Story

Founded by Shubnum Bhopal, Aurora Hands of Hope began as a small community feeding initiative in Durban. Today, we are a registered non‑profit with plans to expand our multi‑purpose upliftment centre to Cape Town. Our work is driven by a deep commitment to restoring dignity and creating opportunity for those left behind.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To empower underprivileged communities by providing food security, accredited skills training, and safe spaces for healing.

Vision: To break the cycle of poverty across South Africa through sustainable, community‑driven programmes.

Our Team

  • Shubnum Bhopal – Founder & Executive Director
  • Community Board Members – Representatives from Phoenix, KwaMashu, and Tongaat
  • Volunteer Coordinators – Dedicated individuals managing feeding and training programmes

Our Impact (2024-2025)

  • 10,000+ meals served
  • 200+ individuals completed skills training
  • 50+ trauma survivors housed
